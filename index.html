Hello!
We are Lewis Transport Limited, a small fictional bus operator, operating local bus services on the roblox platform founded by LewisGaming_RBLX.
We started operations on our first Roblox operation, Layfield, in August 2017 as free drive operator, and later expanded to other operations around 2018/2019.
In around February 2020, this group was formed under different groups until we eventually transferred to the group we use today!
Our CEO has worked in HR roles within other groups and uses his knowledge & mistakes, to keep this group going & improving every day!
